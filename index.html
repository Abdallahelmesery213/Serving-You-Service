Building Maintenance (MEP Services)

From corporate headquarters to industrial, manufacturing and R&D sites, MBM is dedicated to provide the safest, most efficient and cost-effective work environment. Hence some of the largest and most successful corporation in Pakistan entrust the management and operation of their facilities to us. We employ best practices to create and maintain the delivery of superior building maintenance services. MBM act as an extension of our client’s organization to add value based upon our personnel’s expertise, industry knowledge, and the use of cutting-edge technology. MEP stands for Mechanical, Electrical and Plumbing services. We ensure the energy efficiency, management, sustainability and all the MEP services with superior expertise. We provide a complete solution of heating and cooling system, ventilation systems, power system, fire alarm system, lightening protection system, supply of hot and cold water and drainage of waste water system.
